== Inheritance hierarchy ==

	Player
	EnemyBase:
		<ENEMIES>
	ItemBase:
		<ITEMS>
	WeaponBase:
		<WEAPONS>

EntityBase is the base scene for all things that move
GameManager is for component composition and dependency injection

NO PROJECTILE SPEED - just inherit player's linear velocity
